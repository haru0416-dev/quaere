# Hypotheses

Use for root-cause explanations. Each open hypothesis needs a prediction, a falsifier, at least one supporting probe, and at least one disconfirming probe.

## Status values

- open
- confirmed
- rejected
- inconclusive
- fixed
- deferred

## RCA note

- 5 Whys, Ishikawa/fishbone, and fault-tree branches are hypothesis generators, not proof. Record which probe validates each causal step.
- Do not force a single root cause when the evidence supports necessary contributing factors or AND/OR preconditions.
